---
description: Browse the generated profile pages and per-phase run artifacts.
---

Use the `navigate-profile` skill to explore the profile. List runs and
artifacts with `pp_list_runs`, `pp_run_status`, and `pp_list_artifacts`; read a
specific artifact with `pp_load_artifact`; and open the generated markdown under
`profile/context/` (agent pages) and `profile/guide/` (human guide) on request.
